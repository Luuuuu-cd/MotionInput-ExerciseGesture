Purpose and background

According to research, British citizen spends 11.1 hours averagely per week on their health and fitness, that includes 7 hours per week in the gym or working out. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, thousands of British citizens have canceled their gym memberships, as a consequence, home gym equipment (e.g. exercise bikes, rowing machines) become the first choice of workout.

Most home gym equipment involves repetitive motions which may lead to boredom, to overcome this, fitness video games are designed to keep people entertained while promising an intense workout. However, most fitness video games on the market require console and peripherals, furthermore, many peripherals are hard-coded to specific games, which make them cost-ineffective.

The purpose of this project is to examine how can people make use of their current equipment (computer, webcam, home gym equipment) to turn available games into fitness games.
